Cauldron Changes
> Increased recipie output for Elemental catalyst from 1 to 5 per craft
> Increased Qi Gathering of Basic Elemental essences for Wood, Fire, Metal and Water to 20 (+3 range)
> Increased Qi Gathering of Intermediate Elemental Essences for Fire and Water to 40 (+4 range)
> Balanced Cost of Essence materials downwards, standardized requirements of mid tier recipies to make them less frustrating to make.
> Added Beast Cores to Soul Furnace Cauldron Drop Lists to balance out availability of demon blood via the blood cauldron

> Reduced amount of blood crystal refuse required to craft the Ref. Blood Crystal

Auspicious Bundle
	> now returns 12 garbage along with the other items when resources are returned.

7 coloured Scroll
	> Changed means of aquiring Formations and Laws through the 7 coloured scroll. 
		Overall chance has lowered as a result, but the balance should be a bit better. 
		you now have the ability to choose specific laws or formations
		as a side effect, the amount of "rare" materials that the 7 coloured scroll gives has also decreased (for now)

	> Cultivation Law Jade Slip (from 7 coloured scroll): Gives choice of cultivation laws, once learnt, will instead drop random rare resources
	> Formations Jade Slip (From 7 coloured scroll): chance to learn rarer formations, you can pick one... its possible to learn multiple times because different characters can learn formation centers.
	> Expensive Low output, range 5 qi gathering item with enforced 'neutral' attribute - its use should be obvious...
	> Variant Cultivation Platform that fits in a 3x3 cross. (from 7 coloured scroll)
